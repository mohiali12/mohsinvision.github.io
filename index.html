About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a Machine Learning Engineer and Web Developer who's obsessed with turning raw data into intelligent, real-world solutions.

I love building AI-powered applications that not only perform with precision but also look and feel amazing on the web.

My journey revolves around data and innovation - from collecting and cleaning messy datasets to training deep learning models that can recognize patterns, predict outcomes, and make smart decisions.

I've worked with cutting-edge frameworks like TensorFlow, PyTorch, and scikit-learn, building models for image classification, object detection, and predictive analytics that bring real value to users.

But I don't stop at training models - I bring them to life.

Using Flask, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I deploy these AI systems into interactive, responsive web platforms where users can experience the power of machine learning firsthand.

I'm all about creating intelligent, efficient, and user-focused AI solutions - the kind that merge performance with simplicity. I'm constantly exploring new horizons in computer vision, deep learning architectures, and automation to keep pushing the boundaries of what AI can do.

My expertise includes:

  • Languages & Frameworks: Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, OpenCV
  • Data Science: Data preprocessing, feature engineering, model training & optimization
  • Specialties: Computer vision, image classification, ML model deployment
  • Web & Data: Flask, SQL, Matplotlib, Pandas, NumPy
